About AI Hackafest

In the era of fast-changing technological advancement, we aim to harness the benefits of AI while minimizing its negative impacts on human livelihood. AI FOR GOOD – AI Hackafest invites students to explore AI in education and other sectors through hands-on projects and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators with the tools and knowledge to create AI solutions that positively impact society. Through workshops, mentorship, and collaboration, participants will develop practical skills while addressing real-world challenges.

AI Hackafest provides a platform for young minds to transform innovative ideas into tangible solutions, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in artificial intelligence.

About MakerMind

MakerMind is an open innovation competition designed for students who want to turn curiosity into practical impact. Instead of predefined challenges, participants identify real-world problems they care about and propose creative solutions that can improve lives, communities, and industries.

Our Mission

To empower young innovators to combine technology, entrepreneurship, and collaboration in projects that are original, feasible, and built for meaningful impact.

From artificial intelligence and healthcare to climate, education, cybersecurity, accessibility, finance, agriculture, smart cities, and social innovation, MakerMind provides a polished platform for bold ideas to grow.

AI Ethics

Responsible Use of AI

AI tools will be used responsibly as a support mechanism rather than a replacement for individual or team creativity, knowledge, and problem-solving abilities. The submission must reflect the original ideas, technical work, and intellectual contribution of the participant(s).

Excessive reliance on AI-generated content that does not demonstrate genuine understanding or involvement may compromise the integrity of the work. Participants are expected to maintain transparency, originality, and accountability throughout the project process. AI assistance should only be used to enhance productivity while preserving the authenticity of the final submission.

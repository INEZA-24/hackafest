About AI Hackafest

In the era of fast-changing technological advancement, we aim to harness the benefits of AI while minimizing its negative impacts on human livelihood. AI FOR GOOD – AI Hackafest invites students to explore AI in education and other sectors through hands-on projects and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators with the tools and knowledge to create AI solutions that positively impact society. Through workshops, mentorship, and collaboration, participants will develop practical skills while addressing real-world challenges.

AI Hackafest provides a platform for young minds to transform innovative ideas into tangible solutions, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in artificial intelligence.

Eligibility & Requirements

Team Composition

Team of three students from O Level or A Level. Each team must have a designated team leader who will serve as the primary contact.

Project Submission

Submit your AI idea along with a supporting written description. Focus on practical implementation, clear objectives, and ethical use of AI, including fairness, privacy, and responsible deployment.

Focus Sectors

Projects should address challenges in Education, Health, Transport, Security, Environment, or other sectors with social impact potential.

Additional Requirements:

  • Register before January 31, 2026
  • Submit initial project proposal within one week of registration
  • Team of three students (each team must have a team leader)
  • Open to all levels: O Level, A Level
  • Focus sectors: Education, Health, Transport, Security, Environment, Others

AI Usage Ethics Warning

ATTENTION: Inappropriate use of AI tools in project documentation will lead to immediate disqualification.

All project submissions must represent your team's original work. Using AI to generate or substantially complete project content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification from the competition.

Note: AI tools may be used for learning and assistance, but all submitted work must be your own intellectual creation.

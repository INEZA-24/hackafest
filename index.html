About MakerMind

MakerMind is an open innovation competition designed for students who want to turn curiosity into practical impact. Instead of predefined challenges, participants identify real-world problems they care about and propose creative solutions that can improve lives, communities, and industries.

Our Mission

To empower young innovators to combine technology, entrepreneurship, and collaboration in projects that are original, feasible, and built for meaningful impact.

Whether your passion lies in technology, healthcare, education, climate action, or any field where you see a real problem ,MakerMind gives you the platform to propose original solutions, develop them with mentorship, and pitch your work to judges who value creativity, feasibility, and impact.

AI Assistance

AI Usage Ethics Warning

ATTENTION: Inappropriate use of AI tools in project documentation will lead to immediate disqualification.

All project submissions must represent your team's original work. Using AI to generate or substantially complete project content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification from the competition.

AI tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others may support research and productivity but every team remains responsible for originality and must fully understand and explain every part of their solution. Judges may ask detailed implementation questions during evaluation.

Note: AI tools may be used for learning and assistance, but all submitted work must be your own intellectual creation.
